**How to use the replay system!
When on the main menu press "R". This will open up a level select. Enter the level you want to replay and press enter or focus outside.

This will take you to the desired level. On the console can see how many attempts are currently recorded.
The num keys 1-9 will take you to the desired attempt. You can also replay the same attempt or go to the next attempt at the end of level report.

Pressing space or mouse will move the char according to the replay. Notice there are no animations. Pressing P will start autoplay.
Pressing up or down arrow will speed up / slow down the auto play respectively.
